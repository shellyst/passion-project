The Joker

The Clown Prince of Crime




The Joker is easily one of the most infamous, and well-know villains across any form of media, be it comics, film, television, or videogames. His first appearance was in Batman (April 25, 1940). Despite the Joker's characterization as a lunatic clown, he is actually a criminal mastermind, with a warped sense of humour, often responsible for everything from harmless pranks, to dark and sadistic crimes. Throughout his history as a character, he's had many different origin stories, as well as several different characterizations. Having just celebrated his 80th anniversary, the Joker is a timeless character, and a classic villain who will live on in infamy for years to come.



"All it takes is one bad day to reduce the sanest man alive to lunacy. That's how far the world is from where I am. Just one bad day."
